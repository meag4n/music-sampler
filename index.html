Lizzo


David Lee from Redmond, WA, USA via Wikimedia Commons


Lizzo is a 31-year-old American singer, songwriter and rapper. She was born in Detroit, Michigan. She majored in classical music with a specialization in the flute at the University of Houston. She rose to popularity in 2019 with the release of her album "Cuz I Love You," receiving her first nominations in 2019 from award shows such as the People's Choice Awards, BET Hip Hop Awards and the MTV Video Music Awards. You can learn more about Lizzo from her website, lizzomusic.com.
